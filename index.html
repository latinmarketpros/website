Services

Food Tasting:

Demonstrators: Of food and beverages, to promote their products in the market in the best areas and where there is more traffic of consumers. As well as, promote the sale and publicize the products.

Product Sampler:

We offer consumers samples of the products that are being promoted, and we give a brief explanation of what they are, what they consist of or how the products and services that we are promoting are used.

Sales Promoter:

Support Staff:
